Most picked hero in Dota 2



Techies is a ranged hero whose main attribute is Intelligence attribute.

His ability :

  • Proximity Mines

    • Places an invisible mine. Its invisibility cannot be revealed, but it will become visible if the enemy enters the radius of action.

  • Stasis Trap

    • Places an invisible trap that, when triggered, rootes enemy units.

  • Blast Off!

    • Heroes rush into the midst of enemies and, upon landing, detonate their ammo, inflicting huge damage on enemies hit

  • Remote Mines

    • Places an invisible mine that detonates only when manually activated

What is counter pick in Dota 2

Counter-pick is a deliberate choice of a hero as opposed to the heroes of the enemy, to make it easier for yourself to play.

It is important to figure out which hero is better for the draft and not to pick at random. This can make the game much easier for you.
